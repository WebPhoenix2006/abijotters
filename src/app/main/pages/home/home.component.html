Our History

ABI FORTUNE JOTTERS began with Mr. Kay, a visionary who frequently attended workshops and seminars. He noticed that event organizers and trainers were constantly struggling to find quality jotters that were both durable and elegant. These jotters, meant to capture valuable insights and notes, often fell short of expectations, lacking the sturdiness and style that such occasions demanded.

Motivated by this gap in the market, Mr. Kay envisioned a jotter that would not only serve its practical purpose but also stand out as a stylish accessory. With a commitment to quality and design, he founded ABI FORTUNE JOTTERS.
