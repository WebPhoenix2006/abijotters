Our story

Where it began

Since 2021

ABI FORTUNE JOTTERS began with Mr. Kay, a visionary who frequently attended workshops and seminars. He noticed that event organizers and trainers were constantly struggling to find quality jotters that were both durable and elegant. These jotters, meant to capture valuable insights and notes, often fell short of expectations, lacking the sturdiness and style that such occasions demanded.
Motivated by this gap in the market, Mr. Kay envisioned a jotter that would not only serve its practical purpose but also stand out as a stylish accessory. With a commitment to quality and design, he founded ABI FORTUNE JOTTERS. But he didn’t stop there. Understanding the fast-paced nature of events, Mr. Kay ensured that ABI FORTUNE JOTTERS could be custom-designed and delivered within 24-48 hours, offering event organizers the flexibility they needed without compromising on quality.
The premium padded covers, sturdy binding, and smooth, high-quality paper made ABI FORTUNE JOTTERS an instant hit. Event organizers appreciated the quick turnaround time, while participants cherished the jotters, often asking where they could get more.
Thanks to Mr. Kay’s vision, ABI FORTUNE JOTTERS has become a trusted name in the industry. Event organizers and trainers now rely on these jotters not just for their practicality, but for the added value of style and speed. ABI FORTUNE JOTTERS has redefined what a jotter can be—a tool of excellence delivered with precision and care in just 24-48 hours.
